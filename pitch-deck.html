The AI you depend on doesn't know you.

  • They forget what you tell them. Preferences, routines, past decisions. You have to re-explain yourself every conversation.
  • They have limited context. AIs are limited to what they can read in a single session. You have to manually remind and update them.
  • They only know what you relay to them. They can't observe your life, so their insight is limited to what you think to type.
